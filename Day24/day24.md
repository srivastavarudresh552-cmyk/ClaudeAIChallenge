# 🚁 DroneDeliver

**Autonomous Drone-Based Last-Mile Delivery Platform**

## 📌 What is DroneDeliver?

DroneDeliver is a **B2B2C Delivery-as-a-Service (DaaS)** startup building India's first commercially-operated cold-chain pharmaceutical drone delivery platform — targeting Tier-2 cities where refrigerated road logistics fail patients every monsoon season.

We replace cold-chain road vans with **AI-optimised autonomous drones** that deliver in 20–40 minutes with **≥99.5% temperature compliance** at **Rs 35–45 per delivery** — versus the Rs 60–90 road cost that currently dominates.

> **"India loses patients to preventable cold-chain failures every monsoon. We're fixing the last mile from the air."**

---

## 🎯 The Problem

| Pain Point | Who Feels It | Scale |
|---|---|---|
| Cold-chain pharma spoilage (monsoon failures) | Pharma distributors, patients | Rs 5–15/km on refrigerated road delivery |
| Last-mile delivery costs at 40–60% of OPEX | Logistics providers, e-commerce | Rs 60–90/order burning quick-commerce margins |
| Traffic congestion causing 2–4 hour delays | E-commerce brands, end consumers | $22B/year cost to India's logistics sector |
| 600M rural Indians with no reliable delivery | Rural populations, healthcare system | No road = no medicine |

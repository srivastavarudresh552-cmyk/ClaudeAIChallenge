## Observations

### Lazy Prompt Output

* Basic explanation
* Less detail
* Simple design

### Engineered Prompt Output

* More structured
* Better examples
* Professional design
* Easier to understand

## Key Learnings

* Detailed prompts improve AI outputs.
* Context helps AI understand the task better.
* Specifying format and audience leads to higher-quality results.


## Conclusion

Prompt engineering significantly improves output quality and consistency.

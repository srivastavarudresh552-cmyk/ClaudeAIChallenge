# 📊 Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) — Deep Dive Fundamental Research Dashboard

The dashboard consolidates business overview, valuation metrics, financial performance, growth trends, balance sheet strength, ownership structure, peer comparison, and stock performance into a visually appealing investor-focused interface.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📌 Company Snapshot
- NSE & BSE listing information
- Current Market Price (CMP)
- Market Capitalization
- P/E Ratio
- Dividend Yield
- 52 Week High / Low
- Financial Reporting Date

### 🏢 Business Overview
- Company background
- Industry positioning
- Global presence
- Revenue segment breakdown
- Employee strength

### 💰 Financial Analysis
- Annual Revenue
- Net Profit
- Earnings Per Share (EPS)
- Operating Margin
- Revenue Growth
- Free Cash Flow Generation

### 📈 Valuation Metrics
- P/E Analysis
- Relative valuation indicators
- Market pricing insights

### 📊 Growth Analysis
- Revenue growth trends
- Profitability trends
- Business expansion indicators

### 🏦 Financial Health
- Debt-to-Equity Analysis
- Cash & Cash Equivalents
- Net Cash Position
- Interest Coverage Ratio
- Free Cash Flow Quality

### 👥 Ownership Structure
- Promoter Holdings
- Institutional Ownership
- Public Shareholding

### 🔍 Peer Comparison
Compare TCS against major IT sector competitors on:
- Revenue
- Profitability
- Margins
- Valuation
- Market Capitalization

### 📉 Stock Performance Visualization
- Simulated 1-Year Price Trend
- 52 Week High vs Current Price
- Downside / Upside Analysis
